# Reviews and Ratings

Share your thoughts on books with ratings, reviews, and comments in Colibri.

## Rating Books

### Star Ratings

Rate any book on a 5-star scale:

1. Navigate to a work's detail page
2. Click on the star rating widget
3. Select 1-5 stars
4. Your rating is saved automatically

### Rating Scale

- **5 stars**: Outstanding, a favorite
- **4 stars**: Very good, highly recommended
- **3 stars**: Good, worth reading
- **2 stars**: Fair, has issues
- **1 star**: Poor, not recommended

### Viewing Ratings

Your personal rating is displayed:

- On the work detail page
- In the library grid/list view
- In your reading statistics

### Changing Ratings

To update your rating:

1. Navigate to the work's detail page
2. Click a different star value
3. The rating updates immediately

### Removing Ratings

To remove your rating:

1. Navigate to the work's detail page
2. Click the same star value again
3. The rating is cleared

## Writing Reviews

### Creating a Review

1. Navigate to a work's detail page
2. Scroll to the **Reviews** section
3. Click **Write a Review**
4. Enter your review text
5. Optionally add a star rating
6. Click **Post Review**

### Review Content

Your review can include:

- **Text**: Your thoughts on the book (supports Markdown)
- **Rating**: Optional star rating
- **Spoiler marking**: Mark sections that contain spoilers

### Markdown Support

Reviews support Markdown formatting:

```markdown
# Section heading

**Bold text** and _italic text_

- Bullet points
- Are supported

> Blockquotes for emphasis

[Links](https://example.com) work too
```

### Spoiler Tags

To hide spoilers in your review:

```markdown
This book is great! ||The ending is a twist|| and I loved it.
```

Text between `||` markers is hidden behind a spoiler tag.

## Comments

### Commenting on Books

In addition to reviews, you can leave quick comments:

1. Navigate to a work's detail page
2. Scroll to the **Comments** section
3. Enter your comment
4. Click **Post**

### Comment Threads

You can reply to other users' comments:

1. Click **Reply** on any comment
2. Enter your reply
3. Click **Post**

### Comment Reactions

React to comments with emoji:

1. Hover over a comment
2. Click the reaction button
3. Select an emoji
4. Your reaction is added

## Managing Your Reviews

### Editing Reviews

To edit your review:

1. Navigate to the work with your review
2. Click the **Edit** button on your review
3. Make your changes
4. Click **Save**

### Deleting Reviews

To delete your review:

1. Navigate to the work with your review
2. Click the **Delete** button
3. Confirm the deletion

## Viewing Others' Reviews

### Reading Reviews

On any work page:

1. Scroll to the **Reviews** section
2. Reviews are sorted by helpfulness and date
3. Click **Show More** to see older reviews

### Helpful Reviews

Mark reviews as helpful:

1. Read a review
2. Click **Helpful** if you found it useful
3. The review's helpfulness score increases

This helps surface the most useful reviews.

### Filtering Reviews

Filter reviews by:

- **Star rating**: Show only 5-star reviews, etc.
- **Date**: Most recent first
- **Helpfulness**: Most helpful first

## Review Guidelines

### What Makes a Good Review

- **Specific**: Include concrete examples
- **Balanced**: Mention both strengths and weaknesses
- **Constructive**: Explain your rating
- **Spoiler-free**: Use spoiler tags for plot details

### What to Avoid

- **Personal attacks**: On authors or other reviewers
- **Spam**: Promotional content or off-topic text
- **Plagiarism**: Copying reviews from other sites
- **Spoilers**: Without proper spoiler tags

## Privacy and Visibility

### Review Visibility

Your reviews are visible to:

- **Private instance**: Only users on your instance
- **Public instance**: Potentially anyone (if enabled)

Check your instance settings for visibility policies.

### Anonymous Reviews

Colibri does NOT support anonymous reviews. All reviews are attributed to your account.

### Changing Privacy

To make a review private:

1. Edit your review
2. Select **Private** visibility
3. Save changes

Private reviews are only visible to you.

## Moderation

### Reporting Reviews

If you see inappropriate content:

1. Click the **Report** button on the review
2. Select a reason
3. Submit the report

Instance moderators will review it.

### Moderation Actions

Moderators can:

- Remove inappropriate reviews
- Hide reviews pending investigation
- Ban users who violate guidelines

## Reading Statistics

### Your Reading History

View all your reviews and ratings:

1. Click your avatar
2. Select **Profile**
3. Navigate to the **Reviews** tab

### Statistics

See your reading statistics:

- Total books rated
- Average rating you give
- Most-read genres
- Reading over time

## Export and Backup

### Exporting Your Reviews

To export your reviews:

1. Navigate to **Profile > Data Export**
2. Select **Reviews and Ratings**
3. Choose format (JSON, CSV)
4. Click **Download**

This creates a backup of all your reviews.

## Integration with Collections

### Collections from Ratings

Create collections based on ratings:

1. Navigate to **Collections**
2. Click **New Smart Collection**
3. Set criteria: "My rating is 5 stars"
4. Save the collection

This creates a "Favorites" collection that auto-updates.

## Tips and Best Practices

### When to Rate vs Review

- **Rating**: Quick feedback (30 seconds)
- **Review**: Detailed thoughts (5+ minutes)

You can do one, both, or neither.

### Using Reviews for Memory

Reviews help you remember:

- Why you liked a book
- What themes resonated
- Books to recommend to friends

### Review as You Read

Consider writing your review while reading or immediately after finishing for the most detailed feedback.

### Reading Your Own Reviews

Your past reviews are a great way to:

- Rediscover books you loved
- Track your reading preferences over time
- Decide what to reread

## Troubleshooting

### Can't post a review

**Possible causes**:

- Reviews are disabled on your instance
- You don't have permission

**Solution**:

- Contact your instance administrator

### Review not saving

**Possible causes**:

- Network error
- Review too long
- Invalid Markdown

**Solutions**:

- Check your internet connection
- Shorten your review
- Fix Markdown syntax errors

### Can't see others' reviews

**Possible causes**:

- Instance privacy settings
- No other reviews exist

**Solution**:

- Check instance settings
- Be the first to review!
